### Spotify Premium is required

## How to use

- To log in to your Spotify account, press `Play/Pause` button
- Previous Song - `Num 4`
- Play/Pause (or Authorize) - `Num 5` 
- Next Song - `Num 6`
- Reload Blockify - `Num 8`
- Hide Blockify - `Num 9`
- Inscrease Volume - `Num +`
- Decrease Volume - `Num -`
- Toggle In-Game music - `Num 1`
