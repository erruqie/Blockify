### Spotify Premium is required

## How to use

- To log in to your Spotify account, press 5 on NumPad
- Previous Song - Num 4
- Play/Pause (or Authorize) - Num 5 
- Next Song - Num 6
- Reload Blockify - Num 8
- Hide Blockify - Num 9
- Inscrease Volume - Num +
- Decrease Volume - Num -
